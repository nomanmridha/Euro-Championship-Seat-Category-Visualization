# Key Insights Report

## 1. Region-wise Ticket Pricing
- Significant variation in average ticket prices across regions
- Western European regions show higher average prices
- Eastern regions demonstrate higher price sensitivity

## 2. Device-wise Purchasing Behavior
- Desktop users tend to purchase higher-priced seats
- Mobile users dominate volume but at lower average prices

## 3. Country-wise Price Differences
- Host countries show higher average ticket prices
- Football-dominant nations display premium seat demand

## 4. Gender-wise Spending Patterns
- Male customers show slightly higher average spending
- Female customers demonstrate consistent purchasing behavior

## 5. Business Implication
Seat pricing and category distribution can be optimized by region and customer segment
to maximize revenue while maintaining accessibility.
